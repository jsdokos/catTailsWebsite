Donations

By donating to Cat Tails you are helping to support a 501(c)(3) organization focused on providing a no-kill rescue experience for cats in and outside of the Manhattan area.

How to help CatTails:

Paypal Donation

If you wish to donate please use the button to the right.


Amazon Smile

You can use our unique charity link or search for CatTails when you check out.

GoFundMe

You may also donate money through our GoFundMe website.

Dillon's Community Rewards Program

  1. Members must visit visit our website
  2. Sign in OR Create an account
  3. Click on "Enroll Now"
  4. Enter the 5-digit NPO (48924) and search
  5. Select your Organization and click on "Enroll"

Donation to Veterinary Partner

Konza Veterinary Clinic is the Veterinary Partner of CatTails. Donations may be made to them to help offset the veterinary costs that we incur while getting animals ready for adoption. They can be contacted at: 785-776-9111
